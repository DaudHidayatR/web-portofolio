## What it proves

This project represents the infrastructure lab behind my DevSecOps learning practice. It focuses on building repeatable environments where security controls can be tested before being applied to real delivery workflows.

## Focus areas

- Kubernetes-based local lab design for controlled experimentation.
- Service-to-service security concepts with service mesh patterns.
- Secrets management practice with OpenBao and secure configuration habits.
- Documentation-first infrastructure work so experiments can be repeated, reviewed, and improved.

## Why it matters

DevSecOps work needs practical environments, not only theory. This lab gives me a safe place to validate deployment, secrets, and security workflow ideas before turning them into blog posts or reusable project patterns.
